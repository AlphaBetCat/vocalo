# Business Context — Agentic Automation Business

## What This Is
A one-person AI automation agency selling AI receptionist and automation services to local/small businesses nationwide. Built on Claude Code (brain), n8n (orchestration), and GoHighLevel (client platform).

## Founder
Axon — non-technical, based in Utah (Provo/Orem area). Motivated by time freedom, family, health. Comfortable with sales calls. Wants to learn paid ads. Willing to invest upfront time to build a system that runs with minimal involvement.

## Core Vision
Build a business that runs mostly automatically. AI agents handle delivery (receptionist calls, review requests, CRM automation). Axon handles strategy, client relationships, and approving outputs at critical gates. Scale by adding clients, not employees.

## Service Stack — Three Tiers
- **Starter ($497/mo):** 24/7 receptionist service, calls answered, appointments booked, FAQs handled, call transfers, text summary after every call
- **Professional ($697/mo):** Everything in Starter + missed call text-back + lead follow-up sequences
- **Elite ($897/mo):** Everything in Professional + Google Review automation + AI review responses + monthly performance report
- **Future Premium:** Lead Gen + Paid Ads ($1,000+/mo add-on)

Blended average revenue per client ≈ $630/mo. Need ~10 clients for $6K/mo target. 25 clients = $15,750/mo. 35 clients = $22,050/mo.

## Go-To-Market
- Meta paid ads targeting business owners (primary)
- Cold outreach as supplementary channel
- Free trial (10-14 days or 1 month) to land first clients and collect testimonials
- Use own AI receptionist to handle inbound sales calls (eat your own dog food)

## Client Acquisition Strategy
- Ads + landing page created BEFORE starting GHL 30-day trial
- Willing to sacrifice month 1 profit for testimonials and month 2 guaranteed revenue
- No existing audience, network, or warm leads — starting from zero

## Key Platforms
- **Claude Code** — Agent orchestration, content generation, research, ad copy
- **GoHighLevel** — White-label CRM, voice AI, client automations (30-day free trial of Unlimited plan)
- **n8n (local)** — Workflow automation, webhook routing, scheduling, API glue
- **Meta Ads** — Primary paid acquisition channel

## Revenue Targets
- Minimum: $5,000-$6,000/mo (covers family needs + savings)
- Stretch: $50,000/mo (scaling phase)
- Timeline: Profitable by month 2. Willing to invest month 1 in setup, trials, and testimonials.

## What This Is NOT
- Not a social media management agency
- Not a content creation business
- Not a lead gen agency (yet — future phase)
- Not dependent on clients' ability to close leads
