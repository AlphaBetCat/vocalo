## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q:** Do you offer financing?
**A:** Yes, we offer financing through GreenSky with plans starting at 0% APR for qualified buyers. Jake can go over the options during your appointment.

**Q:** How long does an AC installation take?
**A:** Most installations are completed in one day, usually 4 to 6 hours. We'll confirm the timeline during your free estimate.

**Q:** Do you service all AC brands?
**A:** Yes, we service all major brands including Carrier, Trane, Lennox, Goodman, Rheem, and more.

**Q:** Is the diagnostic fee waived?
**A:** Yes, the $89 diagnostic fee is waived if you proceed with the repair.

**Q:** What areas do you serve?
**A:** We serve Austin, Round Rock, Cedar Park, Pflugerville, Georgetown, and surrounding areas within 25 miles of downtown Austin.

**Q:** Do you offer emergency service?
**A:** Yes, we offer emergency service for existing customers. If it's an emergency, let me connect you with Jake right away.

**Q:** How often should I get my AC serviced?
**A:** We recommend a tune-up twice a year — once in spring for your AC and once in fall for your heating system. This helps prevent breakdowns and keeps your warranty valid.
