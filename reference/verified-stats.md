# Verified Stats — For Landing Page & Ad Copy

All stats below are sourced from published research. Use freely in ads, landing pages, sales calls.

---

## Missed Calls at Small Businesses

### 62% of calls to small businesses go unanswered
- **Source:** 411 Locals study (widely cited across industry)
- Home service companies miss 62% of inbound calls
- Professional services miss 54%
- Retail businesses with full-time staff still miss 48%
- **Use in ads:** "62% of calls to local businesses go unanswered. Every one is a lost customer."

### 85% of callers won't call back if you don't answer
- **Source:** Multiple industry studies (Numa, Dialzara, ReviewPoint)
- Only 20% of callers bother leaving a voicemail
- 67% of people admit they ignore voicemails entirely
- **Use in ads:** "85% of customers won't try again. They'll call your competitor instead."

---

## Hold Time / Abandonment

### Average caller hangs up after 2 minutes 18 seconds on hold
- **Source:** Call center industry research (LiveAgent, Nextiva)
- 60% of customers hang up if hold exceeds 2 minutes
- 60% won't wait more than 1 minute
- At 5 minutes, 90%+ hang up
- **Use in ads:** "Your customers won't hold for more than 2 minutes. They'll call someone who answers."

---

## Speed to Lead (Response Time)

### 78% of customers buy from the first company that responds
- **Source:** InsideSales, Harvard Business Review research
- **Use in ads:** "78% of customers buy from whoever answers first. Not the best. The fastest."

### Responding within 1 minute increases conversions by 391%
- **Source:** Kixie, Verse.ai
- 100x more likely to connect if you respond in 5 min vs 30 min
- Leads contacted within 5 min are 21x more likely to convert vs 30 min
- After 5 min, odds of qualifying drop 80%

### Industry average response time is 42 hours
- Best-in-class: under 5 minutes
- **Use in ads:** "Most businesses take 42 hours to respond. We answer in 2 rings."

### 30% of leads are never contacted at all
- **Source:** InsideSales
- **Use in ads:** "30% of leads never get a callback. Don't be that business."

## Cost of Human Receptionist (USA, 2025)

### Average receptionist salary: $37,000-$42,000/year
- **Source:** ZipRecruiter, Indeed, Glassdoor, SalaryExpert (2025)
- Base monthly salary: $3,088-$3,300
- Add benefits + payroll taxes + PTO + training = $4,000-$4,500/month true cost
- **Use in ads:** "A receptionist costs $4,000/month — and still misses calls after hours."
